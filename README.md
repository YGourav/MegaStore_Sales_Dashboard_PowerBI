# Mega STore Sales Analytics & Forecasting Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
This project presents an end-to-end **Power BI Sales Analytics & Forecasting Dashboard** designed to analyze sales performance, identify trends, and support data-driven business decision making. The dashboard focuses on delivering clear insights through interactive visuals and KPI-driven analysis.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance and revenue trends
- Identify top-performing regions, products, and time periods
- Enable interactive exploration using filters and slicers
- Support business stakeholders with accurate sales forecasting and insights

---

## Dataset
- Public sales dataset (CSV format)
- Includes order dates, product categories, regions, revenue, and quantities

---

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Sales Growth Trend
- Top Products by Revenue
- Regional Sales Performance
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales Forecast (Power BI built-in forecasting)

---

## Dashboard Features
- Interactive KPI cards for quick business overview
- Time-based trend analysis for sales performance
- Product and region-wise breakdown
- Slicers for dynamic filtering by date, region, and product
- Forecasting to estimate future sales trends

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Data Analysis
- Business KPI Analysis
- Trend & Time-based Analysis

---

## Key Business Insights
- Identified high-performing regions contributing the majority of revenue
- Observed seasonal trends impacting monthly sales performance
- Highlighted product categories driving consistent growth
- Forecasting indicates potential growth opportunities in upcoming periods
